About Us

Flowers that never fade

We make handmade, crochet flowers for people who want something beautiful to last as long as the feeling that inspired it.

How it started

More Than Fl0wers began with a simple frustration: flowers are one of the most meaningful gifts you can give, but they're gone in a week. We started crocheting our own — first for friends, then for friends of friends — and realized we'd stumbled onto something people actually wanted to keep.

Every piece is still made the same way it was on day one: by hand, one stitch at a time, with yarn chosen for how closely it can mimic the real thing.

What we make

From single statement stems to full custom bouquets, every order on this site is built around the same idea: choose exactly what you want — flower, filler, foliage, color — and we'll craft it to order. Prefer not to decide? Pick a curated template, or tell us the occasion and let us surprise you.

What we believe in

Made by hand

No factories, no machines — every stitch is made by a real person, start to finish.

Built to last

No water, no wilting, no expiration date — these flowers are meant to be kept, not replaced.

Made for you

Every color, flower, and arrangement on this site can be customized to fit what you actually want to say.
